About Me

I got involved in Software Engineering while doing an 8-week online course by Jomo Kenyatta University of Science and Technology(JKUAT). The course inspired great interest in Software Engineering which steered me to pursue a life-long career in it. I am also greatly inspired to invent technology essential to young people just like me and others; to create something that will meet all our points of need. I take pride in my role as a young lady in Software Engineering to encourage other women to look into the same.

Personally, as a lady who loves fashion, I struggle with my outfits sometimes. I used to wish for a site that would advise me on my choice of clothing and accessories when I an unsure. When I ventured into Web development, I decided to become the change I and other women worlwide needed. I developed a website called "The Fashion Advisor" which would come to solve my fashion problems!
